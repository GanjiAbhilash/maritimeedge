Visakhapatnam (Vizag) Customs Updates — April 2026

Vizag Customs April 2026

During April 2026, Visakhapatnam Customs issued multiple operational instructions impacting container terminals at Visakhapatnam Port, ICDs, and regional CFSs. These updates aim to strengthen compliance while ensuring smooth cargo movement along India's east coast.

Infrastructure & Terminal Operations

Customs and port authorities continued efforts to improve cargo evacuation, streamline terminal operations, and reduce dwell time through closer coordination with terminal operators, CFSs, and transporters at Visakhapatnam Port.

CFS & ICD Operational Measures

Operational advisories emphasized:

  • Timely documentation submission
  • Optimized gate operations
  • Compliance with Customs examination and scanning procedures at CFSs and ICDs

Digital & Compliance Enhancements

April 2026 updates reinforced the use of electronic systems for documentation, access control, and auditability to ensure transparency and faster customs clearances.

Trade Facilitation Initiatives

Trade facilitation remained a priority with measures focused on:

  • Reducing transaction costs
  • Improving predictability of customs processes
  • Enabling faster cargo release for compliant trade

Stakeholder Advisory

Importers, exporters, customs brokers, and logistics operators are advised to stay aligned with local customs advisories and port-specific SOPs to avoid delays and non-compliance. Proactive alignment with these updates will help stakeholders maintain smooth cargo flows across ports, CFSs, and ICDs in the Visakhapatnam region.
